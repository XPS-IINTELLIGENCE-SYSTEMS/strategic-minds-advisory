# Content and Avatar System

## Mission

Turn AI work into highly engaging educational media.

The platform should show AI doing useful work, then package the work into YouTube videos, Shorts, TikTok, Instagram Reels, X posts, LinkedIn posts, newsletters, email reports, dashboard lessons, and avatar-narrated explainers.

## Content Rule

Content must be truthful, useful, source-backed where factual, entertaining without deception, risk-aware, and clear about simulation vs reality.

## Avatar Team

### The Risk Manager

Calm, blunt, protective. Handles trading safety, risk, loss prevention, and no-fake-fill discipline.

### The Builder

Practical and tactical. Handles digital business building, tools, workflows, and low-cost startup systems.

### The Skeptic

Witty and evidence-first. Challenges weak claims, identifies traps, and protects viewers from bad assumptions.

### The Creator

Energetic and story-driven. Converts AI work into engaging scripts, thumbnails, shorts, and hooks.

### The Operator

Straightforward and technical. Shows how systems, apps, automations, dashboards, and databases work.

### The Strategist

Big-picture but grounded. Connects trends, opportunities, simulations, and next-best actions.

## Avatar Rules

- disclose AI-generated media
- do not impersonate real people
- do not fake testimonials
- do not fake financial results
- do not claim guaranteed outcomes
- use human review before public publishing

## Content Pipeline

```text
AI run log
-> source receipts
-> lesson extraction
-> script draft
-> avatar persona selection
-> thumbnail prompt
-> short-form cuts
-> human review
-> publish-ready package
```

## Viral Content Principles

Prioritize useful secrets, not fake secrets; strong hooks; clear stakes; real tools; live demonstrations; before/after process; mistakes to avoid; practical next steps; transparent limitations; and unusual but honest insights.
