# AI Labs

## AI Paper Trading Lab

Purpose: teach paper-trading process, market literacy, risk management, source verification, and trade journaling.

Rules:

- simulated only
- no real-money performance claims
- no fake prices
- no fake fills
- no guaranteed profits
- every trade needs entry, stop, target, thesis, invalidation, and position size

Core assets:

```text
SPY
QQQ
BTC
ETH
NVDA
GLD
TACTICAL
CASH
```

Core allocation model:

```text
20% cash reserve
18% SPY
14% QQQ
14% BTC
10% ETH
10% large-cap momentum basket
7% GLD / hedge
7% tactical day-trading bucket
```

Risk rules:

```text
0.5% to 1.25% max risk per trade
2.5% daily stop
6% weekly stop
cash is a position
survival comes before speed
```
